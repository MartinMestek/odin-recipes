Cacio e Pepe

Descripton

Cacio e pepe is the most divine pasta dish made with simple, high-quality ingredients. Freshly cracked black pepper adds tiny spicy bursts of flavor with each bite.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Salt
  • Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Parmigiano Reggiano cheese
  • Heavy creamy
  • Olive oil
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Combine the pasta and sauce
